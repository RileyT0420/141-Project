Band Boosters

Overview

The Band Boosters would like to welcome you to the new school year! The Harrison band program is steeped in a rich history of music excellence. As a band parent, you are also a Band Booster. We collect no fees as a booster organization. We assist the band program through volunteer efforts as well as through raising funds to help pay for sectional teachers, upkeep of marching band uniforms and concert wear, the purchase of band music, and other special needs not covered by the TSC budget. We host the Instrumental District Solo & Ensemble contest annually. We are a large site for this event, so we count on our families for volunteer support at this event. This year, your students will be challenged musically as they participate in a variety of band activities. You will have the joy of watching their lives become richer through playing, performing, and learning about music. Harrison has one of the best high school band programs in Indiana.
